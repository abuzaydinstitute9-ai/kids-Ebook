Chapter 1
Who is Allah?

Before there was anything — no sky, no earth, no stars, no you — there was Allah سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَى. He always was. He always will be. Nothing made Him. Nobody created Him. He is the One and Only God. 🌟

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
Qul huwa Allahu ahad
"Say: He is Allah, the One!" ✨
Surah Al-Ikhlas, 112:1

Chapter 2
How Allah Created Everything

Allah created everything! All He has to do is say كُن — "Kun!" — which means "BE!" And it becomes! SubhanAllah — سُبْحَانَ اللَّه — How Amazing! 🌈

إِنَّمَا أَمْرُهُ إِذَا أَرَادَ شَيْئًا أَن يَقُولَ لَهُ كُن فَيَكُونُ
Innamā amruhu idhā arāda shay'an an yaqūla lahu kun fayakūn
"When He wants something, He only says 'Be!' and it is!" ✨
Surah Ya-Sin, 36:82

Allah created everything in 6 days. Here is what He made:

☁️
Days 1 & 2 — The Heavens
Allah made the sky, the heavens above, and all the space between them
🌍
Days 3 & 4 — The Earth
He made the earth, mountains, rivers, trees, plants, and all the beautiful things we see!
☀️
Days 5 & 6 — Living Things
He made animals, birds, fish, insects — and finally, He made the first human: Adam عليه السلام!
🌿
Say "SubhanAllah!"
Every time you see something beautiful — a flower, a butterfly, the sky — say سُبْحَانَ اللَّه (SubhanAllah) — "How perfect is Allah!" 🌸

Chapter 4
What Allah Asks Us To Do

Because Allah loves us, He gave us rules to keep us safe and happy. These are called Commands. Following them is how we show Allah we love Him too! ❤️

  • 🤲
    Pray to Allah (Salah) We talk to Allah 5 times a day. It's like calling your best friend — but even better! 📞
  • 💛
    Be Kind to Everyone Be kind to your parents, friends, and even animals. The Prophet ﷺ was always kind! 🐾
  • 🗣️
    Tell the Truth (Always!) Allah loves those who are honest. Never lie — even if it's hard! 💎
  • 🤝
    Help Others Share your toys, help carry things, be gentle. That makes Allah happy! 😊
  • ❤️
    Love & Respect Your Parents Allah said: be kind to your parents. They are a big gift from Allah! 🎁
وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ
Wa aqīmu as-salāta wa ātū az-zakāta
"Establish prayer and give to those in need." 🌸
Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:43

Chapter 5
Loving & Fearing Allah

We love Allah because He loves us first! He gave us everything — our life, our food, our family, our smiles! 💛

But we also have deep respect for Allah. This is called Taqwa — تَقْوَى. It means we are careful not to do bad things, because we know Allah is always watching. 👀

❤️
We LOVE Allah
Because He is good, kind, and always there for us
🙏
We RESPECT Allah
Because He is the Greatest — nothing is bigger than Allah!
😇
We OBEY Allah
When we follow His rules, He is pleased and rewards us!
🌈
We TRUST Allah
He always knows what is best for us — even when things are hard
وَهُوَ مَعَكُمْ أَيْنَ مَا كُنتُمْ
Wa huwa ma'akum ayna mā kuntum
"And He is with you wherever you are." 🌟
Surah Al-Hadid, 57:4
💡
Taqwa — تَقْوَى
Imagine Allah is always watching — because He is! 😊 When you want to do something bad, remember: "Would Allah be happy with this?" If not, don't do it! That is Taqwa! ✨

Chapter 6
From Allah We Come,
To Allah We Return

Did you know? Everything starts with Allah and everything ends with Allah! He created us, He takes care of us, and one day — we will all go back to Him. 🌸

إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ
Innā lillāhi wa innā ilayhi rāji'ūn
"Indeed, we belong to Allah and to Him we will return." 🌿
Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:156

This life is like a beautiful journey — but it is not forever. One day, this world will end. Then comes a new life — a much bigger, better world! 🌈

🌱
We Begin With Allah
He created us from nothing and breathed life into us
🌍
We Live on Earth
This life is a test — to do good and love Allah!
🦋
We Return to Allah
After this life, we go back to Allah — like a butterfly going home
🤲 What We Say When Something Sad Happens:
إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ
Innā lillāhi wa innā ilayhi rāji'ūn
"We belong to Allah and we will return to Him."
Quran, Surah Al-Baqarah 2:156

Chapter 7
Jannah & Jahannam

After this life, Allah will be our Judge. He knows every single thing we ever did! He is The Most Fair — الْعَدْلُ (Al-'Adl). He will reward the good and be just with those who did wrong.

🌿
JANNAH
الْجَنَّة
The Garden of Paradise! For those who did good, believed in Allah, and tried their best. In Jannah:
  • 🍇 Delicious fruits always
  • 🌊 Rivers of honey & milk
  • 💎 Beautiful palaces
  • 😊 No sadness, ever!
  • 🌟 Seeing Allah's Light
🔥
JAHANNAM
جَهَنَّم
The fire for those who turned away from Allah and chose to do wrong, again and again. But remember:
  • 🤲 Allah always forgives
  • 💛 If we say sorry & try
  • ✨ Keep doing good deeds
  • 🌿 Choose Jannah every day!
وَأَمَّا مَنْ آمَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَالِحًا فَلَهُ جَزَاءً الْحُسْنَى
Wa ammā man āmana wa 'amila sālihan falahu jazā'an al-husnā
"Whoever believes and does good — they will have the best reward!" 🌟
Surah Al-Kahf, 18:88
🌿
Every Good Act Counts!
Even a smile is a good deed! Saying Bismillah before eating, helping a friend, being kind — Allah writes it all down. Go earn your Jannah today! 💛

Chapter 8
Our Duas to Allah

Dua — دُعَاء — means talking to Allah. You can ask Allah for anything! He hears every word, every whisper, even the thoughts in your heart. 💗

🌙 Dua Before Sleep
اللَّهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا
Allāhumma bismika amūtu wa ahyā
"O Allah, in Your name I sleep and in Your name I wake."
Bukhari
☀️ Dua When Waking Up
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا
Alhamdu lillāhil-ladhī ahyānā ba'da mā amātanā
"All praise to Allah who gave us life after sleep."
Bukhari
🍽️ Dua Before Eating
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
Bismillāh
"In the name of Allah." — Always say this before eating! 🌟
Abu Dawud
