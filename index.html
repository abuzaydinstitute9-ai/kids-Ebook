Chapter 1
Who is Allah?
قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
Qul huwa Allahu ahad
"Say: He is Allah, the One!"
Surah Al-Ikhlas, 112:1

Before there was anything — no sky, no earth, no stars, no you — there was Allah. He always was. He always will be. Nothing made Him. He is the One and Only God!

🤝
Be Kind
To parents, friends, and even animals!
💬
Tell the Truth
Allah loves those who are honest. Always!
🎁
Help Others
Share, help carry things, be gentle!
👨‍👩‍👧
Love Your Parents! 💕
Allah said: be kind to your parents. They are a beautiful gift from Allah! Always obey them and make them happy.

Chapter 8
Our Duas to Allah

Dua means talking to Allah. You can ask Him for anything! He hears every word, every whisper, even thoughts in your heart. 💝

🌙 Before Sleep
اللَّهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا
Allāhumma bismika amūtu wa ahyā
"O Allah, in Your name I sleep and in Your name I wake."
Sahih Bukhari
☀️ When Waking Up
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا
Alhamdu lillāhil-ladhī ahyānā ba'da mā amātanā
"All praise to Allah who gave us life after sleep."
Sahih Bukhari
🍽️ Before Eating
